Scala WebGPU Examples

Simple Triangle

Basic triangle using hardcoded vertex positions in the shader. Demonstrates WebGPU initialization and shader generation.

Buffer Triangle

Triangle with vertex data loaded from GPU buffers. Demonstrates vertex buffers and buffer layouts.

Painter Triangle

Triangle using the Painter abstraction. Demonstrates CanvasApp, Shade, Form, Shape, and painter.draw().

Typed Bindings

Two triangles with type-safe named uniform bindings. Demonstrates compile-time validated bind() with Vec3, Mat2, and Vec2 uniforms.

Painter DSL

Two triangles with shaders written in the Scala DSL. Demonstrates the Program builder with typed expression accessors for vertex and fragment stages.

Panel Triangle

Triangle rendered to an intermediate Panel (render-to-texture), then blitted to canvas. Demonstrates paint() and show().

Panel Layer

Fullscreen animated plasma effect using a Layer shader. Demonstrates layerShade(), aspect-corrected UV, time and resolution uniforms, and onResize().

Panel Tex

Two intermediate panels rendered as textures onto shapes in a final canvas panel. Demonstrates panel-to-texture sampling with nearest and linear samplers.

Blur

Triangle rendered to a panel with multi-pass Gaussian blur post-processing. Demonstrates ping-pong layer chain with auto-injected source textures.

Instances

100 rotating triangles in 3D using shape instances. Demonstrates per-instance typed bindings, panel-level runtime bindings for the shared camera matrix, and instance rendering.

Deferred

Deferred lighting with multiple render targets (MRT). G-buffer panel writes albedo and normals to separate textures, then a lighting layer composites them with an animated directional light.

Mipmaps

Colorful scene rendered with automatic mipmap generation, displayed at 4 different mip levels. Demonstrates mip-enabled panels, textureSampleLevel, and the sampler factory with FilterMode.
